Pre-requisites

It is assumed that you have installed both PHP and PEAR.

It is also assumed that your PHP scripts are correctly interpreted by your web server. In most case it will be probably Apache, but you may also have IIS. PHP_CompatInfo runs both on Windows and *nix.
